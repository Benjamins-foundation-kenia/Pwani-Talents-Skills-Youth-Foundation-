A mission-driven foundation empowering the youth of Mtongwe, Mombasa through structured vocational training and sustainable local development.

Our work focuses on the intersection of skill acquisition and economic resilience.

The Pwani Talents & Skills Youth Foundation serves as a bridge for young people in the coastal region, providing the tools necessary to turn raw creative and technical potential into sustainable livelihoods.

01 / Talent Development

Identifying and refining skills in photography, digital media, and arts to meet modern market demands.

02 / Women's Empowerment

Building structured mentorship pipelines and micro-business resources for young women.
